This page was last updated on 13th July 2025 at 12:30pm

Current:

Taunton Castle Way closure affecting local buses

Taunton Town Centre is today (Sunday 13th July) hosting a festival, and Castle Way is closed to buses.

  • First Somerset Service 21 will be using stand I on the parade, beside Flying Tiger.
  • First Somerset Service 22 will be using the stops at County Hall.
  • First Somerset Service 28 will be using stand H on the parade, beside FatFace.

M5 junction 26 closure, Wellington

Junction 26 of the M5 is currently closed, and the Falcon coach service will not be serving Wellington until it reopens! Traffic in Taunton will also be busier due to junction 25 being an official diversion route.

Service 21 & 21A delays at Dunball

First Somerset service 21 and 21A may experience delays between Bridgwater and Highbridge & Burnham for the duration of 2025 due to roadworks at Dunball roundabout!

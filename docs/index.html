Friday 7th February 2025

Service 54: Taunton to / from Yeovil

The village of Fivehead is blocked, preventing buses from going in either direction. Service 54 is diverting via Ilton, with delays expected!

Service 25, 28A: Norton Fitzwarren

The village of Norton Fitzwarren is blocked, preventing buses from going in either direction, and will not be servied by Service 25 or Sevice 28A. Service 25 is diverting via Cotford-st-Luke and the Minehead road, with delays expected and Service 28A is using the same route!

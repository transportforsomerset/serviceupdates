Service 25, 28A: Norton Fitzwarren

The village of Norton Fitzwarren is blocked, preventing buses from going in either direction, and will not be servied by Service 25 or Sevice 28A. Service 25 is diverting via Cotford-st-Luke and the Minehead road, with delays expected, while Service 28A is using the same route!

Railway closure between Taunton and Bristol / London

The Railway line past Taunton heading towards London and Bristol has been closed due to a police incident!

Many services are either delayed or cancelled.

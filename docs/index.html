Transport for Somerset Service Updates

Updates for Somerset based public transport

Welcome to the Service Updates section of the Transport for Somerset website.

Current:

M5 closure Southbound between J23 & J24 Bridgwater causing delays

The M5 southbound between the two Bridgwater junctions (23 & 24) has been closed due to a police incident, causing delays for services across the board. Service 21 and 21A might experience delays due to heavy traffic through Bridgwater!

Service 21 & 21A delays at Dunball

Buses of Somerset service 21 and 21A may experience delays between Bridgwater and Highbridge & Burnham for the duration of 2025 due to roadworks at Dunball roundabout!

Service 22, 22A, 22B, X22 & 24 delays at Blackdown Garden Centre

Buses of Somerset service 22, 22A, 22B & X22, as well as Stagecoach service 24, may experience delays between Taunton and Wellington until July due to roadworks outside Blackdown Garden Centre along Wellington Road!

Coach services from Megabus, National Express, Flixbus and the Berry's London Superfast service may also experience slight delays.

Previous:

Service 25, 28A: Norton Fitzwarren

The village of Norton Fitzwarren is blocked, preventing buses from going in either direction, and will not be servied by Service 25 or Sevice 28A. Service 25 is diverting via Cotford-st-Luke and the Minehead road, with delays expected, while Service 28A is using the same route!

Railway closure between Taunton and Bristol / London

The Railway line past Taunton heading towards London and Bristol has been closed due to a police incident!

Many services are either delayed or cancelled.

Service 54: Taunton to / from Yeovil

The village of Fivehead is blocked, preventing buses from going in either direction. Service 54 is diverting via Ilton, with delays expected!
